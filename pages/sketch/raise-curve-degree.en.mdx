# Raise Curve Degree

<Reference shortcut="Shift + S" command="Raise Degree" button="Right of Command Bar" buttonIcon="button-raise-degree-curve-face" />

Raise a Curve's degree.

## Basic Usage

1. Select one or more Curves.
2. Press <kbd> Shift + S </kbd>  or click the Raise Degree of Curve or Face button <ButtonIcon type="button-raise-degree-curve-face"/> on the Right of Command Bar to raise the curves' degree by 1.
