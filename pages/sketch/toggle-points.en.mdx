# Toggle Points

<Reference shortcut="" command="Toggle Points" button="" />

Hide or show points on faces, curves, and bodies.

## Basic Usage

1. Select a face, curve, or body you want to hide or show CV points.
2. Type **Toggle Points** in the command pallet and left-click on it.
