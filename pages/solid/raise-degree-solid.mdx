# Raise Degree

<Reference shortcut="Shift + S" command="Raise Degree" button="" />

Edit the surface of a Face or Solid by inceasing surface CVs.

## Basic Usage

1. Select a Face or Solid.
2. Raise the degree of the Face or Solid to whatever sections.
3. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
