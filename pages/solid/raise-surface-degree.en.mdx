# Raise Surface Degree

<Reference shortcut="Shift + S" command="Raise Degree" button="Right of Command Bar" buttonIcon="button-raise-degree-curve-face"/>

Expose a surface's CV and raise its degree.

## Basic Usage

1. Select one or more Faces.
2. Raise the degree of the Face by pressing <kbd> Shift + S </kbd> or clicking the Raise Degree of Curve or Face button <ButtonIcon type="button-raise-degree-curve-face"/> on the Right of Command Bar.
3. Confirm with OK in the Command Dialog or right-click.
